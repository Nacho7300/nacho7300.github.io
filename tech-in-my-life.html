Wearable devices help drug addiction

Drug addiction can be solved with wearable devices because it will warn sponsors when the sponsee is nearby a place with common drug exchanges.

When consuming a drug the wearable will shock the person and send an alert to the sponsor.

The wearable may also send an alert to a family member or a close friend when the person is about to relapse.
